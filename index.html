Our story

We are an FMCG company delivering pure, healthy, and FSSAI-certified edible products like mustard oil and wheat flour. We source fresh wheat and mustard seeds directly from farmers and process them in our advanced plant in Gurugram to produce high-quality Shivam Bhog Chakki Atta and Shivam Mustard Oil. By paying farmers fair prices, we support their livelihood while ensuring freshness for our customers. Unlike traditional supply chains involving middlemen and long storage periods, our approach avoids harmful pesticides and preserves the natural quality of the products.
